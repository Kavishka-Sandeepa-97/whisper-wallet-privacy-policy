# Privacy Policy for WhisperWallet

**Effective Date:** December 12, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

## Introduction

WhisperWallet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WhisperWallet (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the App:

- **Email Address**: We collect your email address when you register for an account to identify you and enable authentication.
- **Financial Transaction Data**: We collect information about your financial transactions, including:
  - Transaction amounts
  - Transaction dates and times
  - Transaction categories (e.g., Food, Transportation, Healthcare, Entertainment)
  - Transaction descriptions
  - Transaction types (income or expense)

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent on features, interaction with app functionality
- **Log Data**: Error logs and diagnostic information for app performance and bug fixes

### Information Collected Through Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

- **Camera Permission**: Used to capture images of receipts for expense tracking. Images are processed locally and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them.
- **Microphone/Audio Recording Permission**: Used to record voice commands for voice-based expense entry. Audio recordings are processed and converted to text, then immediately deleted. We do not store voice recordings.
- **Internet Access**: Required to sync your financial data with our secure cloud database and to use AI-powered features.
- **Storage/Media Access**: Used to read and write receipt images and export financial reports.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Core Functionality**: Create and manage your account, store your financial transactions, and enable expense tracking
- **Financial Analysis**: Generate financial health reports, charts, and insights about your spending patterns
- **AI-Powered Features**: Process your transaction data through AI to provide personalized financial advice and suggestions
- **Voice Recognition**: Convert voice commands to text for quick expense entry
- **Data Synchronization**: Sync your data across devices (if you use multiple devices)
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience
- **Communication**: Send you important updates about the app, security alerts, and service announcements
- **Technical Support**: Respond to your support requests and troubleshoot issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a secure cloud database service. Our servers are located in secure data centers with industry-standard physical and digital security measures.

### How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- **Authentication**: User accounts are protected by secure authentication mechanisms
- **Access Controls**: Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only
- **Regular Security Audits**: We regularly review and update our security practices
- **Secure Storage**: Data at rest is stored in secure, encrypted databases

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

After account deletion:
- Your personal data will be permanently deleted from our active databases within 30 days
- Some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes
- We may retain certain anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes

## AI and Automated Processing

WhisperWallet uses artificial intelligence and automated processing to:
- Analyze your spending patterns
- Generate financial health scores
- Provide personalized financial suggestions
- Process voice commands into text

The AI processes your transaction data to provide insights but does not make automated decisions that significantly affect you without human oversight. You have the right to object to automated processing and request human review of AI-generated advice.

## Voice Data Processing

When you use the voice input feature:
1. Your voice is recorded only while you're actively using the microphone feature
2. The audio is immediately converted to text for transaction entry
3. The original audio recording is deleted immediately after processing
4. We do not store, analyze, or share your voice recordings
5. Voice processing may use third-party AI services that process the audio in real-time without storing it

## Camera and Receipt Data

When you use the camera to capture receipts:
- Photos are taken only when you explicitly use the camera feature
- Receipt images are processed to extract transaction information
- You control whether to save receipt images
- Saved images are stored securely in your account
- You can delete receipt images at any time
